The Lost Vessel (spring 2024)

This game was our first group school project. We made a game set on a floating cargo ship in the midel off the ocean.
Player is a coast guard who boards a ship only to find that the ship is deserted. Then his own boat just disappeared and he must find a way to get out of the ghoast ship.
In this game, we had five coders and four graphic artists in our group. Everyone had their own areas of responsibility and I had AI movement and behavior. I also made audio settings for this game.
